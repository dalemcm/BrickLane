Brick Lane Studios combine high quality equipment with a talented and knowledgeable staff. The studio has been designed to provide a comfortable and professional atmosphere and the staff will strive to make your experience a productive and enjoyable one. The 3,000 sq ft facility is located on the corner of Weir Street and Brick Lane in Paisley town centre, minutes away from the M8 motorway, Gilmour Street Rail Station and Glasgow International Airport. Glasgow city centre is reachable in 15 minutes.
